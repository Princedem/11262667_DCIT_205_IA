Undergraduate Programs

Bachelor of Science in Computer Science

The Bachelor of Science in Computer Science program provides students with a solid foundation in computer science principles. Students learn programming, algorithms, data structures, and software engineering.

Bachelor of Arts in Psychology

The Bachelor of Arts in Psychology program explores the science of the mind and behavior. Students study various areas of psychology, including cognitive, social, and developmental psychology.

Bachelor of Business Administration

The Bachelor of Business Administration program equips students with essential business knowledge and skills. Areas of study include marketing, finance, management, and entrepreneurship.

Bachelor of Arts in English Literature

The Bachelor of Arts in English Literature program delves into the rich world of literature. Students analyze classic and contemporary works and develop strong writing and critical thinking skills.
