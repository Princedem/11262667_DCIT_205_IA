Admission Requirements

Admission requirements for our MPhil and MSc programs may vary. Please review the specific admission criteria for your chosen program on the program's page. In general, common requirements include:

  • Bachelor's degree in a related field
  • Letters of recommendation
  • Statement of purpose
  • Transcripts of previous academic records
